# 🎬 PromptPlay

**PromptPlay** is an AI-powered screenplay development platform designed to revolutionize the way writers create, refine, and manage scripts.  
From initial concept to final draft, PromptPlay empowers creators to produce high-quality content effortlessly with the help of specialized AI agents, powerful tools, and seamless integrations.

---

## ✨ Overview

PromptPlay streamlines the scriptwriting process, combining creativity and AI efficiency.  
Whether you're a beginner or a seasoned screenwriter, PromptPlay helps you ideate, develop, visualize, and perfect your scripts faster and smarter.

---

## 🏗 Platform Architecture

PromptPlay is built around four core components:

- **AI Agents** – Specialized AI assistants for every stage of script development
- **Core Features** – Essential tools and enhancements for scriptwriting
- **User Actions** – Powerful interactions to guide your creative journey
- **Integrations** – Seamless connections to external tools and services

---

## 🧠 AI Agents

| Agent | Description |
| :---- | :---------- |
| **Critique Agent** | Analyze scripts for plot holes, pacing issues, and narrative consistency |
| **Rewrite Agent** | Suggest alternate approaches to scenes, dialogue, and story elements |
| **Prompt Engineer** | Help craft effective prompts for targeted script generation |
| **Visual Director** | Recommend visual compositions and cinematographic ideas |
| **Screenwriter** | Generate full script content based on outlines and prompts |
| **Character Agent** | Build and maintain consistent, vivid character profiles |
| **Dialogue Specialist** | Create natural, character-specific dialogue |
| **Format Compliance Agent** | Ensure scripts meet industry formatting standards |
| **Research Agent** | Gather references, competitor insights, and contextual material |

## 🎯 User Actions

- **Generate Script** – Create new content with AI support
- **Save or Export** – Save projects or export to standard formats (.pdf, .fountain, etc.)
- **Customize Prompt** – Adjust AI instructions to fine-tune outputs
- **Invite Collaborators** – Work together in real-time
- **Create Storyboard** – Visualize scenes based on script text
- **View Analytics** – Track metrics and actionable suggestions
- **Play Audio Preview** – Listen to dialogue with AI voiceovers

---

## 🔗 Integrations

- **Screenplay Parsing** – Import/export in Final Draft, Fountain, and other industry formats
- **Text-to-Speech** – Turn your script into audio with realistic AI voices
- **Stock Image Services** – Power the Visual Moodboard feature
- **Production Budget Estimators** – Help forecast production costs
- **Casting Directory Access** – Discover actors for your characters
- **Payment Gateway** – Manage subscriptions and transactions securely

## 🚀 Getting Started

1. **Create an Account** – Sign up at [PromptPlay](https://promptplay.com)
2. **Start a New Project** – Begin with a blank page or choose a ready-made template
3. **Set Project Parameters** – Choose genre, style, and target audience
4. **Generate Initial Content** – Let AI create your first draft or outline
5. **Refine with AI Agents** – Improve your script with specialized support
6. **Collaborate and Share** – Invite team members or co-writers
