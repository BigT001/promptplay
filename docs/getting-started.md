# Getting Started with PromptPlay

Welcome to PromptPlay! This guide will walk you through the basics of starting your first project.

## Step 1: Create an Account
- Visit [promptplay.com](https://promptplay.com)
- Click **Sign Up** and follow the instructions.

## Step 2: Start a New Project
- Choose a template or start from scratch.
- Define project parameters like genre and tone.

## Step 3: Generate Content
- Use the **AI Script Engine** to create your first draft.

## Step 4: Refine with AI Agents
- Select agents like the **Critique Agent** or **Dialogue Specialist** to enhance your script.

## Step 5: Collaborate and Share
- Invite co-writers or editors to your project.

Happy writing! 🎬
