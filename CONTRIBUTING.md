# Contributing to PromptPlay

First off, thank you for considering contributing to PromptPlay! 🚀  
We are excited to have you as part of our creative and developer community.

## Ways You Can Contribute

- **Bug Reports** – Help us identify issues and areas for improvement.
- **Feature Requests** – Suggest new ideas and enhancements.
- **Documentation Updates** – Improve or add to our documentation.
- **Code Contributions** – Submit patches or features through pull requests.
- **Community Support** – Answer questions and help others in the forum.
